## üîß Troubleshooting Interactions

### If Nothing Seems to Respond
1. **Make sure the window has focus** - Click on the demo window first
2. **Look for the buttons** - They should be visible in the middle area
3. **Check mouse position** - Ensure you're clicking within button boundaries
4. **Watch for feedback text** - Success messages appear below buttons

### If Performance Seems Slow
- **Check FPS counter** - Should target 60 FPS (may be lower in emulation)
- **Close other applications** - Free up system resources
- **Monitor frame time** - Should stay under 16ms for smooth animation

### Expected Button Locations
- **Settings**: Left side, gray button
- **Apps**: Center, blue button  
- **Files**: Right side, orange button
- **Button area**: Look in the middle third of the screen
